ILAHIA COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING AND TECHNOLOGY

Ilahia College of Engineering and Technology (ICET) is an engineering college located in Mulavoor, Kerala, India. It is affiliated with APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU) and offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various fields of engineering and technology. The college is known for providing quality education, modern infrastructure, and a focus on research and development. ICET aims to prepare students for the competitive job market by offering academic programs that integrate theory with practical exposure. The college has well-equipped laboratories, a library, sports facilities, and hostels, creating a conducive environment for learning and overall development. It is also involved in various extracurricular activities, including technical fests, cultural events, and social initiatives, fostering a holistic educational experience.
